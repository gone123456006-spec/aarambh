Privacy Policy

Ohm's - Last updated: 28 May 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Ohm's handles information when you use our English learning app distributed on Google Play and other official channels.

2. Information we collect

We may collect: email address and one-time login codes, profile details you provide (name, region, gender, learning level, phone number), learning progress and reward data, and messages sent in random chat to operate the service.

3. Photos, camera, and microphone

Ohm's requests camera and microphone access only when you use features that need them. We do not access camera, microphone, or photo library in the background.

4. How we use information

We use data to authenticate accounts, deliver courses and games, match chat partners, send OTP emails, improve app reliability, and provide support.

5. Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data may be processed by service providers needed to run the app (for example hosting and email delivery).

6. Children

The app is not directed to children under 13. Users under 18 should use the app with parental or guardian consent.

7. Security and retention

We use reasonable security measures and keep data while accounts are active or as required by legal and operational obligations.

8. Your choices

You can update profile details in-app, revoke device permissions in settings, and contact support for account assistance.
