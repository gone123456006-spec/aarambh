Privacy Policy

Ohm's English Learning — Last updated: 28 May 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Ohm's ("we", "our", "us") handles information when you use our English learning app on Google Play and other official channels.

2. Information we collect

We may collect: email address and one-time login codes; profile details you provide (name, region, gender, learning level, phone number); learning progress, game scores, and daily word rewards; messages sent in random chat; and basic device or app usage data needed for security and performance.

3. Photos, camera, and microphone

Ohm's requests camera and microphone access only when you start features that need them (for example video English practice). We do not access camera or microphone in the background.

4. How we use information

We use data to authenticate accounts, deliver courses and games, match chat partners, send OTP emails, improve app reliability, and provide support.

5. Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data may be processed by service providers needed to run the app (for example hosting and email delivery). Random chat messages are visible to matched learners only during an active session.

6. Children

The app is not directed to children under 13. Users under 18 should use the app with parental or guardian consent.

7. Security and retention

We use reasonable security measures and keep data while accounts are active or as required by legal and operational obligations.

8. Your choices and account deletion

You can update profile details in the app, revoke device permissions in device settings, and delete your account from Contact Us in the app menu. Deletion removes your profile, progress, and associated server data. You may also email support@ohmsapp.com from your registered email.

9. Google Play

When you install from Google Play, Google may collect data under its own policies. Our Google Play Data safety section describes what we collect.
